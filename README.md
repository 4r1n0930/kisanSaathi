# 🌱 KisanSaathi — AI Powered Digital Marketplace for Farmers

## 🏆 Green Tech Hackathon Project — SGSITS

KisanSaathi was developed during the **Green Tech Hackathon at SGSITS** by a team of four members who collaborated to build a complete working solution within **one day**.

The project focuses on leveraging **Artificial Intelligence and modern web technologies** to solve real-world agricultural challenges by empowering farmers with a digital platform to directly connect, showcase, and sell their crops.

---

# 🚀 Project Vision

Farmers often face challenges such as limited market access, dependency on middlemen, lack of transparent pricing, and difficulty reaching verified buyers.

KisanSaathi aims to bridge this gap by creating a digital marketplace where farmers can list their crops, connect with traders, and access AI-powered assistance to improve decision-making.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🌾 Digital Crop Marketplace

* Farmers can create crop listings with important details such as:

  * Crop type
  * Variety
  * Quantity
  * Location
  * Expected pricing

* Provides a platform for direct farmer-to-trader interaction.

---

## 🤖 AI-Powered Crop Analysis

* Integrated AI capabilities to analyze crop quality through images.
* Generates automated quality insights to help farmers present better product information.
* Supports transparent evaluation between farmers and buyers.

---

## 🤝 Smart Trading System

* Enables traders to discover crop listings.
* Supports bidding and contract-based interactions.
* Helps farmers get better market opportunities.

---

## 📋 Digital Inspection Workflow

* Allows verification of crop quality through digital inspection processes.
* Supports image-based verification and documentation.

---

# 🧠 AI Integration

AI was used to enhance the agricultural marketplace experience by:

* Automating crop quality assessment.
